Particles (Core Grammar)

  • は Topic Marker
  • が Subject Marker
  • を Direct Object
  • や And (Incomplete list)
  • に Time / Destination /
  • で Place of Action / Means
  • へ Direction
  • と With / And (Complete list)
  • の Possession ('s)
  • も Also / Too
  • から From / Since
  • まで Until / Up to

Verb Tense Reference (Formal ~masu)

FormEndingExample
Present Affirmative~ますたべます (Eat)
Present Negative~ませんたべません (Don't eat)
Past Affirmative~ましたたべました (Ate)
Past Negative~ませんでしたたべませんでした (Didn't eat)
